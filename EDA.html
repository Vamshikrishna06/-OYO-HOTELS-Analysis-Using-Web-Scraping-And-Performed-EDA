Has_AC vs Has_TV 0.69 Strong positive correlation Indicates that AC and TV often co-occur, likely bundled in mid-range or premium listings. Could be used as a proxy for comfort tier.

Has_Wifi vs Clean_Rating 0.32 Moderate positive correlation Suggests that Wi-Fi availability may influence guest satisfaction, possibly due to convenience or expectations.

grouped = df.groupby(['City', 'Price_Category'])['Clean_Rating'].mean().unstack()
grouped.plot(kind='bar', figsize=(10, 6))
plt.title('Average Cleanliness Rating by City and Price Category')
plt.ylabel('Clean_Rating')
plt.xlabel('City')
plt.legend(title='Price Category')
plt.show()

Insights:

In every city, premium hotels consistently score highest in cleanliness. This validates the assumption that higher-tier properties invest more in hygiene and presentation

Across cities, mid-range hotels maintain moderate cleanliness ratings, often bridging the gap between budget and premium. This segment may offer reliable value without luxury pricing.
